About Me

I have a passion for health and fitness, I enjoy completing obstacle course races in my spare time, working out and playing touch footy. I love my Footy, my beloved Melbourne Storm are my team. I enjoy winding down playing video games or hanging out with my family.
I have been married since 2022 to Nicole, and we have a beautiful daughter Evie who keeps us on our toes!



Employment History

In 2006 I started working for Coles supermarkets as a retail manager controlling various departments as well as the whole store at some points. I was responsible for managing about 100 team members to ensure sales targets were met and customer service was achieved.

I have worked the last 10 years in the RAAF as an Air Surveillance Operator where I have achieved the rank of Non-Commissioned Officer doing various roles such as Simulation Pilot, working with various radar & satelite surveillance systems and doing various humanitarian deployments. I have extensive experience in instructional, training development and assessing roles, including creating fundamental courses from the ground up and spearheading WING wide audits of training standards.



My passion has always been with computers, and for a few years I dabbled around with coding in my spare time, creating apps and websites here and there before deciding this is what I want to do. I decided to go back to studying and do my Diploma of Information Technology: Web Development.

I am now embarking on the next stage of my employment journey as a full stack developer where I hope to help people and businesses fulfil their visions by creating robust platforms for them to get their product out to their target customer base.
